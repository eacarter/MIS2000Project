Russ Louks

Russ Louks joins the School of Business and Economics as a professor of practice of Management Information Systems and the Advisor for the ITOxygen Enterprise team. He is a Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP) from the International Information Systems Security Certification Consortium. He has 29 years of experience in designing, developing and implementing computer systems in manufacturing companies, including 4 years of managing the Ford university sourcing office in Houghton.
